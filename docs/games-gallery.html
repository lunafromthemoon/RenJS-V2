Imaginaria

by Orbis Tertius games

Inspired by the developer’s experiences living in Antarctica for 15 months, Imaginaria is equal parts visual novel, point & click adventure, walking sim and documentary.

'Imaginaria' is a term describing both a night watch and the night watcher. You are in charge of Imaginaria duty tonight, in this godforsaken Antarctic station.

Spiked Heart

by lunafromthemoon

Spiked Heart is the very first RenJS visual novel, made as a birthday gift for a friend. This little silly game ended up setting the bases of what RenJS is today.

Spike is just a cat like any other, until the local fairy came to visit and told him to find his true love, or else!. Is it just a coincidence that the fairy looks exactly like Cereza? Can Spike find his true love? And what is the else?
